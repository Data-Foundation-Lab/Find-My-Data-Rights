Find My Data Rights: Your Data Privacy Rights Search Engine

A lot of people don’t really know about their data privacy rights, and the constantly changing laws and confusing legal language make it hard to keep up.

The Find My Data Rights project was created to cut through this confusion, offering a user-friendly platform that helps you understand and protect your personal data rights.

At its core, this project reflects DFLab’s mission: ensuring that everyone has the tools and knowledge to take control of their data and data privacy.

Find My Data Rights isn’t just a search engine; it’s an initiative for good, and the belief that data privacy is a fundamental human right.
